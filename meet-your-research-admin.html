Meet Your Research Administrator

When preparing to submit an application to an external sponsor (NOT UAlbany Funding Opportunities), one of the first steps we recommend that you take, is to contact Sponsored Programs Administration (SPA). The SPA Office has a seasoned team of Research Administrators (RA) who will assist you in preparing, and submitting your application to the sponsor. This individual will assist students in reviewing and adhering to sponsor, University, and Research Foundation guidelines and policies.

To find the research administrator that can best assist you, search for your department below or contact our office at (518) 437-4550.
